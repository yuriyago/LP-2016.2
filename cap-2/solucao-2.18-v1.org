Define a procedure reverse that takes a list as argument and returns a
list of the same elements in reverse order:

#+BEGIN_EXAMPLE
(reverse (list 1 4 9 16 25))
(25 16 9 4 1)
#+END_EXAMPLE

#+BEGIN_SRC scheme
(define (reverse lista)
  (if (null? lista)
      lista
      (append (reverse (cdr lista)) (list (car lista)))))
#+END_SRC
